OBSERVATIONS 25 DCMR CORRECTIVE ACTIONS
8. - No soap at handwashing sink.No paper towels at handwashing sink. Unit in food prep kitchen lacks supplies and appears unused, dirt and grease accumulation on all surfaces, faucets hard to turn and old equipment observed in the sink at call. Thoroughly clean the unit, keep accessable at all times and supplied with soap and paper towels at all times. Use the facility provided during all food prep.  3001.1 Each handwashing lavatory or group of two (2) adjacent lavatories shall be provided with: (a) A supply of hand cleaning liquid, powder, or bar soap; and (b) A nailbrush at the handwashing lavatory used by employees.  
36. - (CORRECT VIOLATION WITHIN 5 CALENDAR DAYS)Rodent feces ( rat ) observed exterior south west side of establishment, smell of vermin on the interior in basement but unable to observe any evidence of vermin interior of location. Large volume of unused equipment and materials in basement providing possible vermin harborage. Remove all unusable goods, eradicate all vermin by professional means and invoice of service required at follow up inspection call.  3210.1 (c ) The presence of insects, rodents, and other pests shall be controlled to minimize their presence on the premises by: (c) Using methods, if pests are found, such as trapping devices or other means of pest control as specified in sections 3402, 3410 and 3411.  
37. - The basement storage area is unclean and poorly organized, cleaning agents, paints and other non food related items observed stored adjacent to and above food products, containers and food related goods. Seperate all non food items from food related goods, elevate all merchandise above the floor and clean the floor areas with attention to corners and baseboards below and adjacent to equipment. Lift the elevation racks and clean the floor below all the units in basement.  816.1 Except as specified in sections 816.2 and 816.3, food shall be protected from contamination by storing the food: (a) In a clean, dry location; (b) Where it is not exposed to splash, dust, or other contamination; and (c) At least fifteen centimeters (15 cm) or six inches (6 in.) above the floor.  
47. - Clean all the counters, cabinet, racks and equipment in the food prep and basement with attention to the sides and tops. Cease and desist placing metal foil on equipment and counters.  1900.3 Nonfood-contact surfaces of equipment shall be kept free of an accumulation of dust, dirt, food residue, and other debris.N  
52. - Waste food grinder in-disrepair, repair of replace unit. Utilize the disposal for all generated food waste during food production, processing and table waste after customer service.  2719.1 Except as specified in section 2719.2, solid waste not disposed of through the sewage system such as through grinders and pulpers shall be recycled or disposed of in an approved public or private community recycling or refuse facility.  
53. - Repair or replace waste food disposal.(CORRECT VIOLATION WITHIN 45 CALENDAR DAYS)  3200.1 The physical facilities shall be maintained in good repair.  
53. - Old or unused equipment stored in the establishment, remove all unusable materials. The floors, walls and ceilings in the food prep kitchen and the basement require cleaning and or repairs with attention to the basement for repairs to the ceilings. All area are required to be clean, smooth and light in color. If painted after repair only non toxic paint authorized in food establishments.  3201.1 The physical facilities shall be cleaned as often as necessary to keep them clean.
